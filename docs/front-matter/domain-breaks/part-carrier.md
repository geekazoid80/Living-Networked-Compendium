# Part 9: Carrier Transport

This section covers the transport technologies used in service provider and carrier networks: MPLS for traffic engineering and VPN services, segment routing for simplified traffic engineering without per-flow state, EVPN for scalable Layer 2 and Layer 3 VPN services over IP/MPLS, optical transport networking (OTN and SDH/SONET), and MEF standards for carrier Ethernet service definitions.

These topics are relevant to engineers working in ISP, carrier, or large enterprise environments. Familiarity with BGP and IP routing (Parts 2 and 3) is assumed.
